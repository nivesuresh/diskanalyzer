    Navigating through the Disk-Analyzer Prototype
    1. Open up “byType.html” from the Prototype 2 folder
    2. Click on the “Movies 35GB” chunk
    3. Hover over “.WMA” and click on “Show Content”
    4. Look at the list of files that’s available within the chunk and click on “Back” if necessary to go back to the smaller chunks ---
    5. Click on the Frequency icon on the right
    6. Hover over some of the points to see the space taken up by the file displayed ---
    7. Click on the Timeline icon on the right
    8. Look through the files and folders (and note the month/year)
    9. Click on the “...” icon with a line underneath it to go to the “future”. You’ll see July-November 2011 displayed
    10. Click on “Friends S1” folder at the top of July 2011
    11. Look at the list of files that belongs within that folder and click on “Back” if necessary to go back to the future timeline
    12. Close out of the program ---
